From 6/3/2019 to 8/2/2019 the Center for the Study of Neuroeconomics will host the Third Annual IFREE/CSN Summer Research Expereince for Undergraduates. The program will run for nine weeks with a week break from 7/1/2019 -- 7/7/2019, and students will be paid a stipend of $4,000. This program does not provide housing or transportation assistance. The program will be run at CSN on GMU's Arlington Campus in Vernon Smith Hall.
